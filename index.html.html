MAKI

1. Maki Zenin is a central character in Jujutsu Kaisen.

2. She was born into the elite Zenin clan but lacked cursed energy.

3. Because of this, she was treated as inferior within her family.

4. Maki compensates with extreme physical strength and discipline.

5. She uses cursed weapons instead of cursed techniques.

6. Her goal is to dismantle the Zenin clan’s corrupt hierarchy.

7. Maki is cold on the outside but deeply loyal.

8. She shares a tragic bond with her twin sister, Mai.

9. Her growth later makes her one of the strongest fighters.

10. Maki symbolizes strength born from rejection.

Zenin Family Tree

Naobito Zenin: Former head of the Zenin clan and a powerful sorcerer.

Naoya Zenin: Arrogant heir who despised Maki and Mai.

Ogi Zenin: Father of Maki and Mai, obsessed with clan status.

Maki Zenin: Rejected daughter who defied clan traditions.

Mai Zenin: Maki’s twin sister with limited cursed energy.

Jinichi Zenin: Senior clan member loyal to Zenin authority.

Chojuro Zenin: Conservative elder supporting clan rules.

Ranta Zenin: Young sorcerer with paralysis techniques.
